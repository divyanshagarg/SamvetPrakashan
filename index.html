ABOUT US



Samvet Prakashan is working with an objective to provide a platform to the new, aspiring & budding Authors, Writers & Poets as well as to collaborate with the established ones.
We are dedicated to serve society by publishing good & informative books including Fictional & Non-fictional.
Samvet Prakashan is making earnest efforts to offer best literature by publishing all possible forms like Novel, Poetry, Ghazal, Short Story etc.
We are also promoting authors who are penning really wonderful content on Motivation, Politics, Business, Health & Yoga, Lifestyle, Spirituality, Life Philosophy, Cinema, Culture, Art, Travel & other interesting topics inspired from all walks of life. We are inviting our fellow Authors, Writers & Poets; who are willing to publish their books & showcase their writings to the masses, to join hands with us.
So, unleash your diaries & get ready to feature your creation in our books.
For publishing please contact us.
We shall be glad to assist you!
